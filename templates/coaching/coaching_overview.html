Coaching vs. Mentorship: A Critical Difference

Performance Coaching

  • Focus: The Client's Mindset and Potential. We guide you to discover your best path forward.
  • Method: Powerful Questioning. We don't give answers; we help you find resilient, unique solutions.
  • Goal: Sustainable, Internal Transformation. The goal is to make you a more masterful self-leader.
  • Analogy: We help you draw your own map.

Traditional Mentorship

  • Focus: The Mentor's Experience. The advice is based on what worked for someone else.
  • Method: Direct Advice. The mentor provides specific instructions and strategies.
  • Goal: Achieving a Specific Outcome. The goal is to follow a proven, external roadmap.
  • Analogy: You are following someone else's map.
